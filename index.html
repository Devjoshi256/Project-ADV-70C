school library

was established in 1980

The DPS Library is one of the hallmarks of the school. The library houses a wide variety of books and journals. It serves as store house of information related, to prescribed text books and beyond. Its purpose is to inculcate reading habit amongst students.Renowned works of Fiction, Classics, Self Help Books, Science & Technology Periodicals, Journals related to history, culture, exploration along with selected local and national dailies will be housed here. All articles in the library will be carefully scanned and selected to ensure the correct development of the students.

school auditorium

was established in 1982

The school has a huge auditorium with an excellent provision of fresh air entering from various sides. It is a special corner where hustle-bustle is commonplace. The space can accommodate a large number of students of the school at any given point in time. The Auditorium is brought to use for a range of activities, both scholastic and co-scholastic.

  • School Starts For 8 to 12 students

    School will reopen with a difference. All required measures have been put into place to ensure a safe environment. I) Graded opening is planned: Students of classes 9- 12, who wish to meet their teachers must send written permission from their parents to visit school. The same applies to those coming for Science practicals & Cambridge Board Exams. Some of the measures are enumerated below: 1. Mandatory temperature checks - have been ensured at entry points: the school gates and during working hours in the M.I. Room. 2. Entry at the school gates will be monitored - No entry is permitted without face masks. In case of fever or any relevant symptoms, no entry will be permitted. Particulars will be entered in the register for record purposes if required for contact tracing. Hands are to be sanitized with 70% alcohol based hand rub at entry. 3. Regular Sanitization of all Class rooms & campus with solution of Sodium Hypochlorite is conducted throughout the day. Students are advised also to carry their own sanitizers and use them frequently on touching desks, chairs etc. 4. Social Distancing - Parents are requested to keep their wards in their cars and await their turn for entry maintaining norms of social distancing. In case of pickup of students, parents/ drivers are to remain seated inside vehicles while they wait for their wards. Social distancing of min. 2 meters is to be maintained at all times. Classrooms have been organized with 6 feet distancing & exam rooms with 2 mtrs distance. Laboratories are also allowing only 8 -9 students for this reason. 5. Separate Entries have been organized as fewer students will be attending school at a time. As they will be seated at 6 feet distance, in classrooms, the desired distance will be maintained.
